About Brittany

Brittany Grant has been on a life-long spiritual path, journeying through many different esoteric modalities and beliefs. With a strong foundation in metaphysics, she has branched into Wicca, Shamanism,and West African spirituality, as well as, many other tradtions and pathanons. She embraces drums and dance with vigor and has discovered the magic in music; whether it be through trance dance, sound therapy, singing, or toning. Combining many years of learning and involvement with the pagan and spiritual community, Brittany has decided to share her many unique talents and ideals. Brittany’s readings are untraditional and draw from several decks and oracles to help depict a broader spectrum of opposing energies and ideals. She combines shadows and light, with a focus on dichotomy, to help bring together and incorporate dualistic energies that help to create balance in one’s life.
